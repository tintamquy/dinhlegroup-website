About Dinh Le Group

Dinh Le Group, formerly known as Dinh Le Company Limited, was established on January 15, 2001. After 22 years of construction and development, with extensive experience in project investment in industrial zones both domestically and internationally, Dinh Le Group has achieved certain achievements, affirming its reputation and position in the market in Vietnam and internationally.

Annually, we provide the market with approximately one hundred and fifty thousand square meters of warehouses, factories, and ready-built factories for lease and sale, helping to create approximately twenty thousand jobs.

With experience and operational scale from domestic to international, Dinh Le Group provides comprehensive industrial real estate solutions. We offer optimal services to meet the increasingly high demands of the market. Our company focuses on customer service in all real estate resort projects, providing customers with high-end service experiences, from consulting, buying and selling to after-sales services.

Recognizing the growing opportunities for cross-border business between Vietnam and the United States, we established DLH R&D LLC in Bothell, Washington, to serve as our U.S. subsidiary. This expansion allows us to better serve clients looking to invest, expand, or establish operations in either country.

Our Mission

To build a legal, trusted bridge between Vietnam and the United States, facilitating successful real estate investments and business operations through expertise, integrity, and personalized service.

Our Vision

To be the leading cross-border real estate and investment firm, recognized for our commitment to excellence, transparency, and creating lasting value for our clients in both markets.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all we do
  • Trust: We build lasting relationships based on reliability
  • Innovation: We adapt to changing markets and client needs
