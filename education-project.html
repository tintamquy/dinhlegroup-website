The S-Shaped History Learning Space

A distinctive feature of Dinhle College is the history learning area designed in the shape of the letter "S" ‚Äì a sacred symbol representing the land of Vietnam. This innovative space combines water features, hills, trees, and landscape highlights, creating an area that is both aesthetically rich and culturally profound.

Each position along the S-shaped path is planned as a "learning station" where students explore the milestones of Vietnamese history. These stations teach lessons about patriotism, unity, resilience, and Vietnamese identity across different historical periods.

This space not only helps students learn in a vivid, visual way but also ignites national pride, cultivates a decent lifestyle, responsibility, and an appreciation for preserving traditional Vietnamese values. The interactive design encourages exploration and discovery, making history come alive for students of all ages.
