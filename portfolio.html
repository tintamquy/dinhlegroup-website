Dinh Le College - Multi-Level School & Vocational Training Project

Dinh Le Group is continuing to develop the Multi-Level School and Vocational Training Project, providing experiential learning opportunities for students at all levels in Yen Vinh Village, Quang Minh Commune, Hanoi. With a total area of 99,510m¬≤, this project aims to create a modern, comprehensive educational environment.

The project places special emphasis on moral education and character development through a "Living Values Learning Space" model integrated within the school campus. This innovative approach combines academic excellence with character building, preparing students for success in both their careers and personal lives.

Key Features:

  • Multi-Level Education System: Comprehensive educational system covering multiple levels, from primary to vocational training
  • Vocational Training Programs: Practical, hands-on vocational training designed to prepare students for real-world careers
  • S-Shaped History Learning Space: Unique learning area designed in the shape of Vietnam, featuring learning stations that explore the nation's historical milestones
  • Modern Campus Design: Beautifully designed campus combining water features, hills, trees, and landscape highlights
  • Experiential Learning: Hands-on learning experiences that allow students to explore and discover through direct engagement

Harvard University Partnership

Our strategic partnership with Harvard University represents a milestone in our educational initiatives. Harvard, one of the world's most prestigious and respected academic institutions, shares our vision of creating opportunities for knowledge exchange and academic excellence.

Through this partnership, we facilitate:

  • Research Collaboration: Joint research projects addressing critical issues in business, economics, and international relations
  • Academic Programs: Access to Harvard's world-class educational resources and programs for Vietnamese students and professionals
  • Faculty Exchange: Opportunities for professors and researchers to collaborate and share expertise
  • Executive Education: Customized training programs for business leaders and professionals
  • Knowledge Sharing: Seminars, workshops, and conferences featuring Harvard faculty and experts

This partnership enables Vietnamese students and professionals to access Harvard's renowned educational resources while providing Harvard with valuable insights into the Vietnamese market and business environment. The collaboration creates a two-way exchange of knowledge, culture, and expertise that benefits both institutions and their communities.

Our Educational Programs

Dinh Le Group offers a comprehensive range of educational programs designed to meet the diverse needs of students, professionals, and organizations. Our programs combine academic rigor with practical application, ensuring participants gain both theoretical knowledge and real-world skills.

Professional Development

Certification Programs
