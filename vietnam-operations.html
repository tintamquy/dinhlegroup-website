Dinh Le Group - Our Foundation

Established in 2001, Dinh Le Group has been a cornerstone of the Vietnamese real estate and investment landscape. As our parent company, Dinh Le Group has built a strong reputation for integrity, professionalism, and exceptional service over 24 years of operations.

Our deep roots in Vietnam provide us with invaluable insights into the local market, business culture, and regulatory environment. This expertise enables us to serve as a trusted partner for international investors looking to enter or expand in the Vietnamese market.

Vietnam Industrial Platform

Our Vietnam Industrial Platform represents a comprehensive approach to industrial real estate and investment in Vietnam. We leverage our extensive local knowledge and network to provide:

  • Industrial Real Estate: Access to prime industrial properties and development opportunities
  • Market Intelligence: Deep insights into Vietnamese market trends and opportunities
  • Regulatory Expertise: Understanding of local regulations and compliance requirements
  • Local Partnerships: Strong relationships with local developers, suppliers, and service providers
  • Investment Facilitation: Support for foreign investors entering the Vietnamese market
